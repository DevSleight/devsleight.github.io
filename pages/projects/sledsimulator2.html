Project Overview

Sled Simulator 2 is the follow up to Sled Simulator and is also powered by the Roblox Engine. Sled Simulator 2 was created to improve the codebase from Sled Simulator and make new features such as trading and players having multiple of the same type of item with different properties possible.

Sleds

Players get a personal garage where they can change their equipped parts and show off their sleds to the other players. The garage can have different camera angles depending on the type of part the player is changing.

Parts

Sled Simulator 2 items all have unique ids instead of being unlockable. This has the advantage of allowing trading, properties per part and players can own multiple of each part. Example of these properties are if the part is painted or has a special effects.

Upgrading sleds

In this game the upgrades are tied to the player and map they are currently on, not the sled parts themselves. After adding enough parts to Sled Simulator most of them were cosmetic and kept the best upgrade players had. I wanted to keep this mechanic for the second game and now every part is cosmetic.

Cheaters

In Roblox the default characters are client authorized and to avoid players using that to their advantage their character is cloned and the copy is what sits in the sled. The players real character are instead teleported out of sight and frozen in place. All the stats on the leaderboards in the game are set by the server and it should not be possible for them to be manipulated by the player.
